TapeBoy Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

TapeBoy respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses and how it is used.

Apple Music Access

With your permission, TapeBoy may access Apple Music information using Apple's MusicKit framework, including:

  • Currently playing song
  • Artist name
  • Album name
  • Album artwork
  • Playback status

TapeBoy only accesses this information after you grant permission.

How Information Is Used

The information is used solely to provide the app's functionality, such as displaying your currently playing music and related artwork.

TapeBoy does not upload your music library, listening history, or playback information to any server operated by the developer.

Personal Information

TapeBoy does not collect personally identifiable information.

TapeBoy does not sell your personal information.

Data Storage

App settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

TapeBoy uses Apple's MusicKit framework to access Apple Music content after you grant permission.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be published on this page.
