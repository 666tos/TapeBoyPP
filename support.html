Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I see my music?

Make sure you've granted Apple Music permission in Settings and that you have an active Apple Music subscription.

Why isn't the widget updating?

Widgets update according to iOS system policies. Opening the app occasionally helps refresh displayed information.

Why isn't album artwork displayed?

Some tracks or services may not provide artwork, or the artwork may not be available in your region.
